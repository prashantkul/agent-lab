Agent Lab Course Material Review Program

Welcome to the Agent Lab Course Material Review Program. By participating as a reviewer, you will have early access to course materials that are still in development. This access comes with important responsibilities.

1. Confidentiality Obligations

As a reviewer, you agree to keep all course materials, including but not limited to:

  • Lecture slides, PDFs, and documentation
  • Code exercises, starter repositories, and solutions
  • Homework assignments and grading criteria
  • Any proprietary methodologies or techniques presented

strictly confidential and not share, distribute, copy, or reproduce them in any form without explicit written permission from the course administrators.

2. Permitted Use

You may use the materials solely for the purpose of:

  • Completing the review exercises
  • Providing constructive feedback to improve the course
  • Personal learning and professional development

3. Feedback and Contributions

Any feedback, suggestions, or improvements you provide may be incorporated into the course materials. You grant UCLA Extension and the course instructors a non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use your feedback.

4. Duration

This confidentiality obligation remains in effect for a period of two (2) years from the date of acceptance, or until the materials are publicly released, whichever comes first.

5. Acknowledgment

By clicking "I Accept" below, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by the terms of this Confidentiality Agreement.
